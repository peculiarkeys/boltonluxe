
## UI Design Rule: Soft Typography
Avoid using pure black (`#000` or `text-black` or `text-zinc-900`) or overly bold fonts (`font-bold`, `font-extrabold`) for headers and text. Instead, use softer grays like `text-zinc-700` or `text-zinc-800` and opt for `font-medium` or `font-semibold` to keep the design smooth, elegant, and premium.
